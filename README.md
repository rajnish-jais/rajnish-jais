### About

Staff Engineer at [CloudBees](https://www.cloudbees.com), building the CloudBees Platform — a cloud-native CI/CD and software delivery platform used by enterprises worldwide.

I specialize in distributed systems, Kubernetes-native infrastructure, and high-throughput backend services in Go. I care deeply about **observability**, **correctness**, and keeping systems simple under pressure.

Outside of work I contribute to open source — primarily in the CNCF ecosystem.
